## Thematic Buckets

- **meditation_core** — Terms describing meditative states and practices (samādhi, dhyāna, nirodha, dhāraṇā, saṃyama, ekāgratā, etc.)
- **mind_psychology** — Terms describing the mind and its processes (citta, vṛtti, saṃskāra)
- **affliction_obstacle** — Terms describing what obstructs practice (kleśa, avidyā, abhiniveśa, etc.)
- **practice_discipline** — Terms describing the practitioner's effort (abhyāsa, vairāgya, pratyāhāra, prāṇāyāma, kriyā-yoga)
- **insight_knowledge** — Terms describing wisdom and discernment (prajñā, viveka-khyāti, viveka)
- **liberation_metaphysics** — Terms describing the goal and metaphysical framework (kaivalya, puruṣa, svarūpa)
- **advanced_compound** — Multi-member compounds requiring layered analysis (dharmamegha-samādhi, nirodha-pariṇāma, samādhi-pariṇāma)
